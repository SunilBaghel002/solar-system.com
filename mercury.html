Mercury is the innermost and smallest planet of the Solar System. Because it has almost no atmosphere to retain heat. Mercury's surface experiences the greatest temperature variation of all the planets.

OBSERVATION

Mercury appears as a morning or evening star from Earth, but due to it's proximity to Sun, it is very hard to see. Mercury can be observed at least twice a year: in spring at dusk and in autumn berfore dawn.
